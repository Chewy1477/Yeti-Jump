Welcome to Yeti Jump!
The goal of the game is to collect 10 points. 
Gold coins give you 1 point while the moving red coins subtract 5 points and a life.
This game is similar to Doodle Jump and has the character jump on platforms on a moving screen.
Jumping off the sides of falling below the screen will subtract one of your lives. 
You have three lives.
Space to jump and arrow keys to move.
